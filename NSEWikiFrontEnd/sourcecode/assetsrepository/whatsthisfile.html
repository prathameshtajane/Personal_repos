What's This ? This is Something really amazing

Some solid leading copy will help get your users engaged. Use this area to come up with something real nice. Know what I'm sayin?

“Let’s change the world...one line of code at a time.” We exclaimed as my friend and I gladly shook hands as we found out a solution for a mission critical production issue. We were working since last 72 hours, trying to modify and identifying impacts of different solutions to achieve maximum with minimum code changes. Our back was still hurting, barely allowing us to move. We made the most painful walk to a whiteboard which was completely filled with scribbling from whole market machine architecture to PT architecture and then to the business impacts and at the end having our own solution with minimal code changes .Our perseverance and hard work for the past 72 hours finally paid off. Drained by the endeavour of finding the most efficient solution with no business impact to trading members, we discerned the importance of having a central knowledge repository in the field of trading development in this business world that is changing rapidly. This burgeoning knowledge repository continuously seeks mercenaries’ for sustaining and fuelling its phenomenal growth.

We believe that having a trading knowledge and business logic is as important as coding knowledge. Hustling to only acquire coding knowledge falls short in its scope of capturing the essence of problem solving, while marvellous imagination and business logic embraces the real art of using technology to solve real world problems. Fuelled by this belief, we decided to create our own knowledge repository which will have all the information that we have accumulated over years and years of research and development.

I believe, a Master’s degree from Northeastern University in the field of Computer Science with focus on AI is the logical progression to further expand my horizons in AI and Computer Science in general. The illustrious faculty members like Javed Aslam,Carls Brodley and excellent sterling at Department of Computer Science at NEU, make its graduation program quintessentially suited to my professional and research goals. Carls Brodley’s work on basic machine learning research in developing better methods for constraint-based clustering, multi-task learning, active learning will also provide me an opportunity to explore the obscure area of machine learning in human computer interaction. I feel excited about the burgeoning realm of science and technology, where the focus shifts toward fusing technology with human intelligence to create an age of Artificial Intelligence. I want to contribute to that age of evolution. With my dedication, enthusiasm and perseverance I am confident to live up to Northeastern University’s expectations and also make noteworthy contributions to its outstanding legacy.
